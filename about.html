About Us

Institutional advisory with singular accountability

Unidhan Insurance Marketing (IMF) is built to deliver disciplined, multi-institution advisory for families and enterprises seeking clarity across risk, investment and liabilities.

Our History

Unidhan IMF was established to bridge the gap between product distribution and true advisory outcomes. We began with a simple mandate: deliver structured, documented guidance that helps clients make confident financial decisions.

Vision

To become the most trusted institutional advisory platform for Indian families and businesses seeking multi-institution financial clarity.

Mission

Provide unbiased advisory across risk protection, investments and liabilities with accountability, documentation and consistent review discipline.

USP

Multi-insurer, multi-lender access combined with a single accountable advisory team, ensuring every recommendation is aligned to outcomes, not product quotas.

Advisory Philosophy

Independent, documented, review-led

We believe advisory should be institutional in mindset and personal in delivery. Each recommendation begins with an understanding of your goals and progresses through structured analysis, documentation and periodic review. Our role is to simplify complexity and provide clarity across protection, investment and liability decisions.

Core Values

  • Integrity and independence in every recommendation
  • Structured reviews with documented reasoning
  • Accountability to outcomes and service continuity
  • Client confidentiality and data discipline
  • Long-term partnership mindset
